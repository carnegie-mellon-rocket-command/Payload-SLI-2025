Teensy 4.1

Link to CAD files:
https://grabcad.com/library/teensy-4-1-2

One of my favorite microcontrollers.
Just wish it had better deep sleep and idle capabilities.

Manufacturer site:
https://www.pjrc.com/store/teensy41.html

Please support Paul since he has contributed heavily into the development of this board and lots of Teensy and Arduino libraries.

Has a fast clock speed of 600 MHz.
Can be overclocked to 1000 MHz using air cooling.
It has dedicated float point units.
An ethernet controller and connection.
Lots of pins have PWM capability.

Specifications:
ARM Cortex-M7 at 600 MHz
Float point math unit, 64 & 32 bits
7936K Flash, 1024K RAM (512K tightly coupled), 4K EEPROM (emulated)
QSPI memory expansion, locations for 2 extra RAM or Flash chips
USB device 480 Mbit/sec & USB host 480 Mbit/sec
55 digital input/output pins, 35 PWM output pins
18 analog input pins
8 serial, 3 SPI, 3 I2C ports
2 I2S/TDM and 1 S/PDIF digital audio port
3 CAN Bus (1 with CAN FD)
1 SDIO (4 bit) native SD Card port
Ethernet 10/100 Mbit with DP83825 PHY
32 general purpose DMA channels
Cryptographic Acceleration & Random Number Generator
RTC for date/time
Programmable FlexIO
Pixel Processing Pipeline
Peripheral cross triggering
Power On/Off management
